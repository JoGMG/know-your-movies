Project KYM
Features
The Search By Feature

This feature enable users to search for a movie by movie name or cast name.

The Movie Reviews Feature

There is a review container below every movie, just above the footer, which displays the reviews of that particular movie. The cool thing is it automatically changes every 5 secs and stops on hover. There is also a previous and next control button.

About

We all love movies, whether our team has a greater love for movies is a topic up for debate. Although one of us prefers Korean movies, our shared love for films motivated us to focus on movie casts. This solution is influenced by the vast and complex nature of the movie industry today. We think providing a full movie service can confuse users and make their experience bad. Our goal is to create a platform that is easy for users to navigate and find movie cast information.

This is an ALX SE Foundations Portfolio Project.
